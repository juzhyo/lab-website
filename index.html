Welcome!

We are a research group led by Prof. Goki Eda in the Department of Physics/Chemistry and Centre for Advanced 2D Materials. Our research focuses on fundamental and applied science of novel low-dimensional materials and aims to exploit them in diverse areas of technology from electronics to energy harvesting. Please visit our Research page for details of our activities.
